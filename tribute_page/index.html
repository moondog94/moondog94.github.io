Stan Lee: The most famouse Marvel Comics writer


Stan Lee joined the comic book industry during the Golden Age of comics. However, he did not make his big debut until the Silver Age with the creation of the Fantastic Four in 1961 with the help of the legendary artist Jack Kirby. During his time at Marvel Comics he has been creditted with the creation of the following comic book series:

  • The Amazing Spider Man
  • Fantastic Four
  • The Incredible Hulk
  • The X-Men
  • Avengers
  • Dr. Strange
  • Daredevil
  • Hawkeye
  • Iron Man
  • Ant-Man
